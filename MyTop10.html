My Top 10 Favorites Movies

Bohemian Rhapsody

A biopic of Freddie Mercury and Queen, showcasing their rise to fame, Mercury’s struggles, and their legendary Live Aid performance.

Hacksaw Ridge

The true story of Desmond Doss, a pacifist medic who saved 75 soldiers in WWII’s Battle of Okinawa without carrying a weapon.

The Amazing Spider-Man

The Amazing Spider-Man (2012) follows Peter Parker as he gains powers, falls for Gwen Stacy, and battles the Lizard while uncovering his past.

The Greatest Showman

The Greatest Showman (2017) is a musical drama about P.T. Barnum's rise to fame, starring Hugh Jackman. Filled with dazzling performances and hit songs, it celebrates ambition, acceptance, and spectacle.

One Piece Film: Red

Luffy and his crew attend Uta’s concert, but they soon uncover her hidden powers and a plan that could trap the world in an eternal illusion. As secrets about her past and connection to Shanks unfold, a fierce battle begins.

All The Bright Places

Violet and Finch, two troubled teens, form a deep bond while helping each other heal, but Finch’s inner struggles take a heartbreaking turn.

The BFG

Sophie befriends the Big Friendly Giant (BFG) and joins him on a magical adventure to stop man-eating giants from threatening the human world.

Avatar: The Way of Water

Jake Sully and Neytiri seek refuge with the Metkayina clan as humans return to conquer Pandora. As they adapt to ocean life, an old enemy resurfaces, leading to a fierce battle to protect their family and home.

Life Of PI

Life of Pi is a novel by Yann Martel about a boy who survives a shipwreck and shares a lifeboat with a Bengal tiger, exploring faith and survival.

Fast X

Dominic Toretto battles Dante Reyes, the vengeful son of a past enemy, in a high-stakes, globe-spanning showdown.
